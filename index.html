Classic Homemade Pancakes

Fluffy, golden, and perfect for weekend breakfasts.

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 0.5 tsp Salt
  • 1 large Egg
  • 0.75 cup Milk
  • 2 tbsp Melted Butter

Instructions

  1. Mix Dry Ingredients

    In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

  2. Whisk Wet Ingredients

    In a separate bowl, beat the egg, then whisk in the milk and melted butter.

  3. Combine & Batter

    Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Mix gently just until combined (it is totally fine if the batter has a few lumps!).

  4. Cook Until Golden

    Heat a lightly oiled skillet over medium heat. Pour batter onto the skillet. Cook until bubbles form on top, then flip and cook until golden brown on both sides.
